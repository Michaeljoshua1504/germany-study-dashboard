✈️ Exhaustive Post-Admission Checklist
From receiving your offer letter to arriving in Germany. Your progress is saved automatically.
Phase 1: Acceptance & Enrollment (Immediately after admission)
  • Accept the offer letter on the university portal
    Usually must be done within 7-14 days of receiving the email.
  • Pay the Semester Contribution Fee (Semesterbeitrag)
    Transfer via regular bank transfer or Instarem/Wise. Keep the receipt.
  • Sign up for German Public Health Insurance
    Choose TK, AOK, Barmer, or use Expatrio/Coracle value packages.
  • Request M10 Certificate transfer to your university
    Your insurance provider must digitally transmit your M10 status to the university.
  • Complete Digital Enrollment (Immatrikulation)
    Upload payment receipt and finalize enrollment on the university portal.
Phase 2: Financials & Blocked Account
  • Open a Blocked Account (Sperrkonto)
    Use Fintiba, Expatrio, or Coracle.
  • Transfer the required €11,208 (plus buffer)
    Always add an extra €100-200 for international transfer fee fluctuations.
  • Download the Sperrkonto-Bestätigung
    This is the official Blocked Account Confirmation Document required for the Visa.
Phase 3: Visa Application (Student Visa - Section 16b)
  • Book a VFS Global / Consulate appointment
    Do this ASAP. Slots fill up months in advance for the winter intake.
  • Draft Motivation Letter and update CV
    Align your LOM strictly with your DevOps career goals in Germany.
  • Print & organize all documents strictly to Embassy checklist
    Offer letter, APS, Blocked Account, Insurance, Transcripts, IELTS. Prepare 2 sets of copies.
  • Attend Visa Interview & submit passport
    Be prepared to answer technical questions about your master's modules.
Phase 4: Relocation & Post-Arrival (Germany)
  • Sign a housing contract (WG-Gesucht, Studierendenwerk)
    Beware of scams. Never transfer deposit before signing a legitimate contract or viewing.
  • Pack apostilled/notarized copies of all degree documents
    Essential for university physical enrollment and future job applications.
  • Post-Arrival: City Registration (Anmeldung)
    Must be done at the local Bürgeramt within 14 days of moving into your permanent address.
  • Post-Arrival: Open a German Bank Account (Girokonto)
    Link this to your Blocked Account to receive your €934 monthly payouts.
  • Post-Arrival: Apply for Residence Permit (Aufenthaltstitel)
    Book appointment at the Ausländerbehörde before your 3-month entry visa expires.
